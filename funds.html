Funds & Organizations

Name Details
BBG Ventures BBG Ventures is an early stage fund focused on consumer internet and mobile startups with at least one female entrepreneur.
Belle VC BELLE Capital USA, LP is an early stage angel fund focused on building great companies in underserved capital markets across the USA. We look for capital efficient companies with a unique product or service filling an urgent market need. We target the digital/mobile/internet (IT), technology-enabled products and services, life sciences/medical devices/health IT (Digital Health), and CleanTech market sectors. Companies seeking our capital must have at least one female founder or C-level exec, and/or be willing to recruit top female talent to the C-suite and Board of Directors. We prefer teams with strong domain expertise, and proven commercialization and entrepreneurial experience.
The Vinetta Project The Vinetta Project is a influential ecosystem that supports high growth female founders in taking their businesses to the next level.
Outbox Incubator Outbox Incubator is an initiative by Stemettes to provide seed funding, intensive mentorship, and support to talented girls aged 22 and under who have innovative business and technology ideas
New York Fashion Tech Lab The New York Fashion Tech Lab is a community-driven, relationship building, collaboration & business development platform.
Plum Alley Plum Alley Investments is a private membership that gives its members access to influence innovation and shape the future by investing in private companies. They offer memberships to investors and to entreprenuers.
Women Working Wonders Fund The mission of Women Working Wonders Fund is to fund programs that empower women and girls to effect positive change in the community. Founded and funded by women in 2004, the Women Working Wonders Fund provides grants each year to non-profit organizations for programs that impact the lives of women and girls.
Women's Start-up Lab Women’s Startup Lab is a founder accelerator program. WSLab’s collaboratory of women entrepreneurs empowers founders to be strong leaders, create impactful startups and to influence and shape the world though their startups, innovation and community collaboration. WSLab focuses on female founders and their teams. Located in the heart of Silicon Valley, our non-traditional accelerator focuses on CEO development and coaching in addition to company growth and network expansion.
Merge Lane Merge Lane is a startup investment accelerator for high-growth companies with at least one female in leadership.
The Refinery The Refinery, an early stage accelerator program designed to assist innovative women-led ventures in becoming scaleable and investible businesses. The Refinery engages local intellectual and financial capital to participate in the growth of new businesses, while leveraging community resources.
